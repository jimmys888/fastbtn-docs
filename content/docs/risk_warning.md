## Trading Risk Warning

Trading financial instruments involves **significant risk** and is **not suitable for everyone**.

Prices can move rapidly, and losses may occur in a very short time.

---

## No Guarantees

FastBTN and FastSTAT:
- Do not guarantee profits
- Do not prevent losses
- Do not improve trading performance by themselves

Your trading results depend entirely on:
- Your strategy
- Your risk management
- Market conditions

---

## Leverage and Volatility

Many trading instruments involve **leverage**, which can amplify both profits and losses.

High volatility can cause:
- Rapid drawdowns
- Slippage
- Missed executions

You should fully understand how leverage works before trading.

---

## Technology and Execution Risks

Trading platforms rely on technology.

Potential risks include:
- Internet connection loss
- Platform freezes or crashes
- VPS or hardware failure
- Broker execution delays

FastBTN and FastSTAT cannot prevent or control these events.
